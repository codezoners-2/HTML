short history of HTML

  • in 1989 physicist Tim Berners-Lee wrote a memo proposing an internet-based hypertext system while working at CERN
  • he specified HTML and wrote the first client & server in late 1990
  • proposed it as a system for scientists to use and share documents
  • based it on SGML (another markup language)
  • the hypertext links were his invention

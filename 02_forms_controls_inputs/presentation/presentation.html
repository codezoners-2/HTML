HTML

Form Structure

<FORM NAME="MyForm" ACTION="/handle-form/" METHOD="POST">
  <!-- ... -->
  <INPUT TYPE="SUBMIT" VALUE="Do it!">
</FORM>

All forms have an action, a method, and a submit button.

Method can be GET or POST.
